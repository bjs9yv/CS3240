SecureContact


Welcome to SecureContact. This is a web app that lets you securely share and view reports. You may use the tabs above to navigate the site.

The Reports tab allows you to upload and view your own reports. The Search tab allows you to view public reports by searching by keyword. The Messages tab provides for both encrypted and unencrypted messages. The Groups tab lets you join and manage groups of anonymous users. Lastly, the Account tab allows you to manage your account settings.
